MALEFICENT

Maleficent is a fantasy film that reimagines the classic tale of Sleeping Beauty from the perspective of its iconic villain. The story explores Maleficent’s transformation from a kind-hearted fairy into a powerful figure consumed by betrayal and revenge. With stunning visuals and emotional depth, the film shows that even villains can have untold stories and hidden reasons behind their choices.

TRAIN TO BUSAN

Train to Busan is a South Korean action-horror film that follows a group of passengers trapped on a speeding train during a sudden zombie outbreak. The story combines thrilling survival moments with emotional depth, focusing on the bond between a father and his young daughter. With intense action sequences and a heartfelt core, the film stands out as one of the most impactful zombie movies of recent years.

FALL

Fall is a survival thriller that tells the story of two best friends who climb a remote 2,000-foot radio tower, only to find themselves stranded at the top with no way down. As fear, exhaustion, and desperation set in, the film delivers a gripping experience filled with tension and breathtaking visuals. It’s a story about friendship, courage, and the terrifying limits of human endurance.

ME BEFORE YOU

Me Before You is a romantic drama that follows the heartfelt relationship between Louisa, a cheerful young woman, and Will, a wealthy man left paralyzed after an accident. As Louisa becomes his caregiver, their bond grows in unexpected ways, bringing both joy and emotional challenges. The film explores themes of love, hope, and the difficult choices that shape our lives.

PEREGRINE'S HOME

Miss Peregrine’s Home for Peculiar Children is a dark fantasy adventure directed by Tim Burton. The story follows a boy named Jake who discovers a hidden orphanage where children possess extraordinary abilities, protected by the mysterious Miss Peregrine. Blending gothic atmosphere with imagination, the film explores themes of belonging, courage, and the magic of being different.

FIVE FEET APART

Five Feet Apart is a romantic drama that tells the story of Stella and Will, two teenagers with cystic fibrosis who must maintain physical distance to stay safe. Despite the six-foot rule, they form a deep emotional connection, navigating love, illness, and the challenges of life-threatening conditions. The film highlights the strength of human connection and the courage to love against the odds.

THE MAZE RUNNER

The Maze Runner is a thrilling science-fiction adventure that follows Thomas, a young man who wakes up in a mysterious maze with no memory of the outside world. Alongside a group of boys trapped in the maze, he must uncover secrets, face dangerous creatures, and find a way to escape. The film combines action, suspense, and mystery, making it a gripping start to the dystopian saga.

ALADDIN

Aladdin is a live-action musical fantasy film that retells the classic tale of a street-smart young man who discovers a magical lamp and befriends the powerful Genie inside. As he navigates love, adventure, and the challenges of royalty, Aladdin learns about courage, honesty, and the true meaning of friendship. With vibrant visuals and catchy music, the film brings the beloved story to life for a new generation.

ALICE IN WONDERLAND

Alice in Wonderland is a fantastical adventure directed by Tim Burton that follows Alice as she returns to the whimsical world she first visited as a child. Filled with eccentric characters, magical landscapes, and imaginative challenges, the film explores themes of self-discovery, courage, and the power of imagination. Burton’s signature visual style brings this beloved story to life in a vibrant and surreal way.
