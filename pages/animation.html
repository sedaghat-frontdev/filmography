BOSS BABY

The Boss Baby is an animated comedy film directed by Tom McGrath. The story follows a suit-wearing, briefcase-carrying baby who teams up with his older brother, Tim, to stop a plot by the CEO of Puppy Co. The film blends humor, family dynamics, and heartwarming moments, highlighting the importance of sibling bonds and teamwork.

THE INCREDIBLES

The Incredibles is an animated superhero film produced by Pixar and directed by Brad Bird. The story follows the Parr family, each member with unique superpowers, as they try to live a normal suburban life while secretly fighting crime. When a new villain threatens the world, the family must come together to save the day.

101 DALMATIANS

101 Dalmatians is a classic animated family film produced by Walt Disney. The story follows Pongo and Perdita, two Dalmatian parents, as they try to rescue their stolen puppies from the villainous Cruella de Vil. Filled with adventure, humor, and memorable characters, the film highlights themes of courage, love, and the importance of family.

BIG HERO 6

Big Hero 6 is an animated superhero film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. The story follows Hiro Hamada, a young robotics prodigy, who forms a team of heroes including his inflatable robot Baymax to combat a mysterious villain threatening San Fransokyo. Blending action, humor, and heartfelt moments, the film emphasizes friendship, innovation, and the power of teamwork.

FINDING NEMO

Finding Nemo is an animated adventure film produced by Pixar and directed by Andrew Stanton. The story follows Marlin, a clownfish, as he embarks on a journey across the ocean to rescue his son, Nemo, who was captured by a diver. Along the way, Marlin meets memorable characters and learns about trust, courage, and letting go.

CINDERELLA

Cinderella is a classic animated fairy tale film produced by Walt Disney. The story follows Cinderella, a kind and gentle young woman mistreated by her stepmother and stepsisters, who receives magical help from her Fairy Godmother to attend the royal ball. With themes of kindness, hope, and perseverance, the film showcases timeless animation, memorable music, and the magic of dreams coming true.

MULAN

Mulan is a classic animated action-adventure film produced by Walt Disney. The story follows Fa Mulan, a brave young woman who disguises herself as a man to join the Chinese army in place of her ailing father. Through courage, intelligence, and determination, she becomes a hero while honoring her family.

SCOOBY0-DOO

Scooby-Doo is a live-action/animated comedy-mystery film based on the classic Hanna-Barbera cartoon. The story follows the Mystery Inc. gang—Fred, Daphne, Velma, Shaggy, and their talking dog Scooby-Doo—as they solve a spooky mystery at a haunted island resort.

RATATOUILLE

Ratatouille is an animated comedy-adventure film produced by Pixar and directed by Brad Bird. The story follows Remy, a talented rat with a passion for cooking, who dreams of becoming a great chef in Paris. When he forms an unlikely partnership with a young kitchen worker, they work together to create culinary masterpieces.
