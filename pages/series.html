13 REASONS WHY

13 Reasons Why is a teen drama series that begins with the story of Hannah Baker, a high school student who leaves behind a series of tapes explaining the reasons for her tragic decision to end her life. As her classmates uncover the secrets within the recordings, the show explores heavy themes such as bullying, mental health, friendship, and responsibility.

THE RAIN

The Rain is a Danish post-apocalyptic series that follows siblings Simone and Rasmus as they struggle to survive in a world devastated by a deadly virus carried through rainfall. Six years after the outbreak, they join a group of survivors in search of safety and answers. Blending suspense, drama, and sci-fi elements, the show explores themes of survival, trust, and the fragile hope of rebuilding humanity.

STRANGER THINGS

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s, blending supernatural mysteries with nostalgic small-town life. The story begins when a young boy goes missing in Hawkins, Indiana, leading his friends and family to uncover a secret world known as the Upside Down.

PEAKY BLINDERS

Peaky Blinders is a British crime drama set in post–World War I Birmingham. The series follows Thomas Shelby and his notorious family gang as they rise to power through ambition, violence, and political maneuvering. Known for its gritty atmosphere, sharp dialogue, and powerful performances, the show combines historical events with stylish storytelling, making it one of the most acclaimed crime dramas of the decade.

TEEN WOLF

Teen Wolf is a supernatural drama series that follows Scott McCall, a high school student whose life changes after being bitten by a werewolf. As he learns to balance his new abilities with the struggles of teenage life, Scott faces dangerous creatures, hidden secrets, and the challenges of friendship and love. Mixing horror, action, and romance, the show became a cult favorite among young adult audiences.

QUEEN’S GAMBIT

The Queen’s Gambit is a limited drama series that tells the story of Beth Harmon, an orphaned chess prodigy who rises to fame in the male-dominated world of competitive chess during the Cold War era. As her talent grows, she struggles with loneliness, addiction, and the pressure of success.

SQUID GAME

Squid Game is a South Korean survival drama that follows hundreds of desperate contestants who enter deadly children’s games for the chance to win a massive cash prize. Beneath its thrilling and violent challenges, the series explores themes of inequality, greed, and human morality. With its gripping story and striking visuals, Squid Game became a global sensation and one of the most-watched Netflix shows of all time.

GAME OF THRONES

Game of Thrones is an epic fantasy series based on George R.R. Martin’s novels, set in the mythical land of Westeros where noble families vie for control of the Iron Throne. Blending political intrigue, war, and supernatural forces, the show is known for its complex characters, shocking twists, and grand scale. Despite its controversial final season, it remains one of the most influential and talked-about TV series of all time.

RIVERDALE

Riverdale is a teen drama and mystery series inspired by the classic Archie Comics characters. Set in the seemingly quiet town of Riverdale, the show uncovers dark secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists as a group of teenagers navigate friendship, love, and danger. Mixing romance, suspense, and a touch of noir style, the series became a popular blend of high school drama and mystery.
