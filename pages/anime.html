SPIRITED AWAY

Spirited Away is a Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It follows Chihiro, a young girl who becomes trapped in a mysterious spirit world after her parents are transformed into pigs. As she works in a magical bathhouse to find a way to free them, Chihiro discovers courage, resilience, and the power of kindness.

PONYO

Ponyo is a Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. The story follows a goldfish named Ponyo who dreams of becoming human after befriending a young boy named Sosuke. As Ponyo’s magical transformation disrupts the balance of nature, the two friends embark on a heartwarming adventure filled with wonder and discovery.

MARINE

When Marnie Was There is a Japanese animated drama film produced by Studio Ghibli. The story follows Anna, a lonely girl sent to the countryside for her health, who befriends a mysterious girl named Marnie. As their friendship deepens, Anna uncovers secrets about Marnie and herself, leading to emotional growth and self-discovery.

YOUR NAME

Your Name is a Japanese animated romantic fantasy film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The story follows two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies and experience each other’s lives. As they try to understand this strange connection, a deeper bond forms between them, leading to a journey filled with love, memory, and fate.

WEATHEERIN WITH YOU

Weathering With You is a Japanese animated romantic fantasy film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The story follows Hodaka, a runaway teenager in Tokyo, who meets Hina, a girl with the mysterious ability to control the weather. As they navigate love, hardship, and supernatural events, their bond grows while facing challenges that could change their lives forever.

KIKIS

Kiki’s Delivery Service is a Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. The story follows Kiki, a young witch who moves to a new town to begin her training and starts a delivery service using her broomstick. Through her adventures, Kiki learns about independence, friendship, and self-confidence.

ARRIETTY

The Secret World of Arrietty is a Japanese animated fantasy film produced by Studio Ghibli and directed by Hiromasa Yonebayashi. The story follows Arrietty, a tiny girl who lives secretly under the floorboards of a human house, and her friendship with a boy named Sho. As their worlds collide, Arrietty learns about courage, trust, and the importance of connection.

LA TUMBA

Grave of the Fireflies is a Japanese animated war drama directed by Isao Takahata and produced by Studio Ghibli. The story follows two siblings, Seita and Setsuko, struggling to survive in Japan during World War II. As they face hunger, loss, and the harsh realities of war, their bond and love for each other remain the heart of the film.

MARRY

Directed by Hiromasa Yonebayashi and produced by Studio Ponoc, this enchanting animated film is based on Mary Stewart’s 1971 novel The Little Broomstick. It follows Mary Smith, a curious young girl who discovers a mysterious flower called “fly-by-night,” granting her magical powers for a single night. Her adventure leads her to Endor College, a school for witches, where she uncovers dark secrets and faces moral dilemmas.
